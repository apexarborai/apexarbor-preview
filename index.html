About Us

An Independent Platform
Backed by Affirma Capital

Apex Arbor Holding Limited is a globally-oriented industry-focused investment company headquartered in Singapore, with Affirma Capital as a shareholder. It serves as an independent investment platform with strong partnership with Affirma Capital, focusing on cross-industry and cross-geography investments, integrating AI-driven intelligent decision-making with a rigorous governance framework. Affirma Capital — formerly Standard Chartered Private Equity — has invested over USD 5.8 billion across 99 companies with 59 full exits, and its investment philosophy and governance standards are deeply embedded in Apex Arbor's approach.

Regulated by the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS), Apex Arbor upholds a world-class governance framework inherited from Standard Chartered Bank — the institutional foundation of its shareholder — with ESG principles embedded in every investment decision. We deploy capital with disciplined deal structuring, strong minority protections, and an intense focus on value creation.
